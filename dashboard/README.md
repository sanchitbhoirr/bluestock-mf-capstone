## 🎯 Objective

The primary goal of this dashboard is to democratize complex financial data. By translating millions of rows of raw NAV and transactional data into intuitive visualizations, it empowers decision-makers to track industry growth, evaluate fund performance, and understand investor demographics at a glance.

## 🛠️ Dashboard Architecture (The 4 Pages)

The .pbix file is structurally divided into four comprehensive analytical views:

### Industry Overview

Features high-level KPI cards tracking Total AUM (₹81L Cr), Monthly SIP Inflows (₹31K Cr), and Folio Counts. It includes trend lines and bar charts mapping the dominance of top AMCs.

### Fund Performance

Integrates our custom 0-100 Fund Scorecard. It utilizes scatter plots to visualize the Risk vs. Reward (Return vs. Volatility) paradigm, allowing users to identify top-tier funds instantly.

### Investor Analytics

Focuses on behavioral and demographic data. It breaks down transaction volumes geographically (State-wise & City Tier) and visualizes the distribution between SIP, Lumpsum, and Redemptions.

### SIP & Market Trends

A macro-economic view using dual-axis charts to correlate monthly SIP inflows with the Nifty 50 Benchmark, alongside category-wise heatmaps.
